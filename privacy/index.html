Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how HabitDex ("App"), operated by Charles Tananbaum ("we", "us", or "our"), handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Data stored on your device:

  • Habit names, categories, and completion logs
  • Virtual pet data (creatures, levels, stardust balance)
  • User profile and preferences
  • Streak and achievement data

This data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not transmit or store this data on our servers.

AI Coaching sessions:

  • If you use the AI Coaching feature, your session prompts are sent to a third-party AI service to generate responses. We do not store coaching conversation history on our servers beyond what is needed to deliver the response.

Purchase data:

  • In-app purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information (credit card numbers, billing address, etc.).

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising
  • We do not sell, share, or rent any personal information to third parties

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit: Processes all in-app purchases and subscription management. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Push Notification Service: Delivers habit reminders and streak notifications. No personal data is shared beyond the notification content.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your habit and profile data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).

5. Data Retention

Since your data is stored locally on your device, it persists until you delete the App or clear its data. Deleting the App removes all locally stored data. Purchase history is retained by Apple in your Apple ID account.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete. You can delete all locally stored data at any time by deleting the App from your device. You can manage your subscription and purchase history through your Apple ID settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at charles@totail.ai.
